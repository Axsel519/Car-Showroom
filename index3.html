Ferrari Purosangue Model 2024

Hard work brings valuable achievements

The 2024 Ferrari Purosangue is the first four-seat SUV in Ferrari history. Like any Ferrari, it is extremely capable and powerful, but it offers a wonderful balance between high performance and high luxury. It is powered by a naturally aspirated V12 engine mounted in the front center that generates up to 715 horsepower, and it has a very luxurious cabin of heavy caliber with a touch of the sporty character that highlights the identity of Ferrari cars.

The Ferrari Purosangue stands out for its elegant design and smooth aerodynamics. The front end features linear LED daytime running lights with adaptive LED night headlights and automatic high beams. This is in addition to a black grille decorated with the Ferrari pony. The elegant sides come with integrated door handles and 22-inch wheels at the front and 23-inch wheels at the rear. The color of the brake calipers can be customized, and the rear doors are suicide doors. The tailgate is electric, with a kick function for automatic opening.
